ABOUT

Resort to Reason is a publishing and educational platform founded and curated by Spyros Tserkis, dedicated to exploring a wide range of philosophical and scientific ideas. It features carefully selected excerpts from historical works alongside newly written essays.

Each essay and excerpt is assigned a dedicated Archival Resource Key (ARK) persistent identifier. Both are offered in downloadable PDF and accessible online formats, ensuring ease of reading across devices. To broaden reach and improve discoverability, they are also republished on Substack.
