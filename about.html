ABOUT US

Resort to Reason is an online platform dedicated to exploring a broad spectrum of philosophical and scientific ideas, all inspired by the timeless Enlightenment motto: Sapere aude â€” dare to know. The essays published here are intended for a general audience, including readers without specialized training in science or philosophy, and therefore use as little technical terminology as possible. The content of the essays reflects solely the author's personal perspective.

Each essay is available both as a downloadable PDF and in an accessible online format for ease of reading across devices. Additionally, the essays are republished on Substack to reach a wider audience and enhance discoverability. Engaged readers are warmly encouraged to share their thoughts and critical feedback. Comments, questions, or reflections can be sent directly to the author of the essay or at the platform's contact page.
